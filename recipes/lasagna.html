Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Prepare Meat Sauce
    1. Cook Ground Beef until browned
    2. Add in Onions and Garlic and saute until soft
    3. Stir in Tomato Sauce
    4. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Prepare Cheese
    3.
    1. Grate mozzarella cheese
    2. Grate parmesan cheese
  3. Prepare Lasagna Noodles
    4.
    1. Boil Lasgna noodles in water until soft
    2. Empty water and pour Lasgna Noodles into the cooked Sauce
