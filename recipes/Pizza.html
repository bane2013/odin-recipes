Cheese Pizza

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with our classic burger. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of flavors and textures, from the juicy beef patty to the crisp lettuce and tangy pickles. Our handcrafted burger buns are toasted to perfection, adding a delightful crunch to every mouthful.

This dish is ready to be served at Jas's Diner, but here is the recipe for yourself to make at home!

Located at: 15365 56 Ave Surrey, BC, Canada

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Cheese slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare Burger Patties
    2.
    1. Divide ground beef into equal portions and shape into patties.
    2. Season patties with salt and pepper.
    3. Cook patties on a grill or skillet until desired doneness is reached.
  2. Assemble Burgers
    3.
    1. Toast burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
    2. Layer bottom half of each bun with lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles.
    3. Place cooked burger patty on top of the veggies.
    4. Top each patty with a slice of cheese.
    5. Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of each bun.
    6. Place the top half of the bun on the cheese.
